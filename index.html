About Me

I was born and raised in Mumbai, India and I am an artist pursuing my Bachelor's degree at Fordham University, Lincoln Centre based in New York. As a multidisciplinary creative, my work focuses on crafting impactful and immersive experiences that bridge the worlds of art and technology.

With a background that spans digital and physical design, front-end development, data collection, and brand management, I am driven by the challenge of blending technical precision with artistic vision to create spaces that resonate and inspire.

My career has spanned diverse fields, including marketing, producing, stage management, and theatre and art restoration.
